Friday, March 29
Pico Mountain Summit
🏔️
⛅ Weather is everything on Pico. Have March 30 in mind as a backup mentally — whale watching and diving can flex if needed.
🥾🥾🥾 2,351m summit · 4–5h up · 3–4h down · Full day
Start 06:00–06:30 from the trailhead Trailhead: Montanha do Pico Nature Reserve entrance, end of ER3 road — about 25min drive east from Madalena, signposted. There's a small car park and the ranger/guide station at the base (~1,200m). Guide is mandatory above 1,950m — book well in advance.
Full day — nothing else planned
Cella Bar in the evening optionalOnly if you have legs left

Whale Watching — 3–4h tour, departing 09:00–10:00 from Lajes do Pico Sperm whales year-round; late March may bring blue whales, sei whales, dolphins, occasional orcas
